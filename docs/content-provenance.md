# Content and Trademark Provenance Checklist

The original repository contained a 310-item static question bank with questions traceable verbatim to published DECA/MBA Research exams. That bank was removed on July 20, 2026. The replacement is a deterministic, committed bank of 7,000 stable items—1,000 in each of seven written-exam families—based only on a Deca Pal-authored general-business and personal-finance concept catalog. Automated checks inspect every item for schema errors, duplicate IDs and stems, quota and objective coverage, prohibited source references, and overlap with a non-expressive Bloom filter of the removed stems. The test feature selects blueprint-weighted sets from this bank and does not send test content to an AI provider. The records are marked `automated-validated`; complete human editorial review is still outstanding.

The repository still contains performance-indicator phrases, event names, rubric terminology, and educational explanations whose source or license has not been established item by item. Do not assume educational use makes third-party material free to copy or redistribute.

Before a public or paid launch:

- Inventory each remaining dataset and substantial passage, including `app/performanceIndicators.tsx`, event/rubric data, concept blueprints, and AI prompt templates.
- Record its author, source URL or document, acquisition date, copyright owner, license or written permission, permitted uses, attribution requirements, and modification restrictions.
- Replace, license, or remove content whose rights cannot be demonstrated.
- Have counsel review nominative use of DECA names and marks, domain/branding, and the independent/unaffiliated disclaimer.
- Add a documented process and contact for copyright or trademark complaints.
- Verify that AI-generated scenarios do not falsely present themselves as official competition materials.
- Review every new or revised test item for factual quality and accidental source similarity; immediately remove disputed content while a complaint is investigated.

The product UI and Terms state that DECA Pal is independent and AI output is unofficial. Those notices reduce confusion but do not replace content rights or trademark review.
